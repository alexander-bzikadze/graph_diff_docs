generate_graph()[source]¶

Generates graph.

Number of node has geometric distribution with node_number_expectation as its expectation. Labels have triangular distribution with (1, (node_number - 1) / 5, node_number) parameters. Edges between all nodes are generated with probability 1/2. :return: graph
